If you are searching for a place that is impeccably designed and includes a range of satisfying facilities, you have come to the right place.

OUR STORY




The Village began as a hidden gem, passed down through generations of a local island family who cherished its untouched beauty and deep connection to nature. Originally a quiet fishing haven, the island was known among locals for its serene beaches and vibrant coral reefs. Years ago, inspired by a dream to share this paradise with the world while preserving its natural charm, the family transformed their ancestral land into a boutique resort. Every villa, path, and palm tree has been thoughtfully placed to honor the island’s heritage and respect its environment. Today, The Village stands as a testament to that legacy—offering guests a chance to experience island life with the warmth, authenticity, and soul of a place built from love and tradition.
