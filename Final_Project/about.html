OUR STORY




The Village began as a hidden gem, passed down through generations of a local island family who cherished its untouched beauty and deep connection to nature. Originally a quiet fishing haven, the island was known among locals for its serene beaches and vibrant coral reefs. Years ago, inspired by a dream to share this paradise with the world while preserving its natural charm, the family transformed their ancestral land into a boutique resort. Every villa, path, and palm tree has been thoughtfully placed to honor the island's heritage and respect its environment.
