How to Fold a Paper Airplane

Introduction |Materials | Folding Steps | Tips

Introduction

Folding a paper airplane is easy and fun! Follow these steps to create your own airplane.

Materials Needed

  • 1 sheet of paper
  • A flat surface to fold the paper

Folding Steps

  • Lay the paper on a flat surface.
  • Fold the paper in half vertically.
  • Unfold the paper and fold each of the top corners into the center line.
  • Fold the top edges again to the center.
  • Fold the plane in half lengthwise.
  • Fold the wings down, matching the top edges of the wings with the bottom edge of the body.

Extra Tips

  • Make sure your folds are as sharp as possible.
  • This airplane has stiff wings and can tolerate fast throwing speeds.
  • Throw it as hard as you can for maximum distance and speed.
